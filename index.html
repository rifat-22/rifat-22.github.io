About

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of North Texas, working under Dr. Mark Albert in the Biomedical AI Lab. My research interests lie in health informatics, clinical NLP, applied machine learning, and bioinformatics, with a focus on turning unstructured clinical notes into structured signal for patient care. Before this, I spent three and a half years as a software engineer at Dynamic Solution Innovators Limited and Inovace Technologies Limited in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
